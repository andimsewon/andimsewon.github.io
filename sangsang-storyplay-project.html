Sangsang StoryPlay ‚Äì AI-Driven Interactive Storytelling Platform for Children

Click to view presentation

An interactive AI storytelling system that integrates child-speech recognition, generative story creation, and developmental feedback analysis.

This project reimagines traditional, passive story consumption into an active, child-led narrative experience where the child becomes the main character and drives the story forward. By combining a generative AI story engine, a child-optimized speech recognition model, and linguistic/emotional development analytics into a unified workflow, the platform enables creative play, language learning, and emotional growth in a single educational experience.

The AI Story Engine processes the child's spoken input, generates new scenes in real time, and branches the storyline based on the child's choices and responses. Children can select roles, speak their ideas, and influence how the story unfolds. Unlike fixed storybooks, each session produces a unique narrative, continuously stimulating imagination and expanding expressive skills.

A core capability is the integration of ASR (Automatic Speech Recognition) and generative AI for real-time interaction. A speech model tailored to the vocal patterns of children aged 4‚Äì7 converts spoken input into text, which the story engine uses to construct the next scene and respond through character voices (TTS). The system also analyzes vocabulary variety, sentence complexity, emotional cues, and creative responses to automatically generate developmental reports for parents.

The user experience is structured into three stages:

  • Story Creation: Role selection ‚Üí child speech input ‚Üí real-time scene generation ‚Üí next prompts
  • Interaction Logging & Analysis: Tracking lexical diversity, new word usage, emotional expressions, and creative branching
  • Parent Dashboard: Visualized growth reports covering language development, emotional tendencies, and preferred story themes

All interaction data is uploaded and synced through cloud services, with immediate updates to growth charts and storytelling history. The core story-generation module and voice-interaction engine are designed as extendable components, enabling future expansion into multilingual story generation, smart-speaker integration, or educational institutional versions (B2B).

This platform demonstrates a novel model of active, AI-mediated learning for children, combining generative AI, speech interaction, and developmental analytics. It moves beyond traditional storytelling to support imagination, linguistic growth, emotional expression, and parent-child engagement within a single, scalable system.
