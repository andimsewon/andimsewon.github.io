Jeonbuk National University

I am an undergraduate student in Computer Engineering at Jeonbuk National University, specializing in Natural Language Processing, Large Language Models, and Embodied AI systems. Currently, I serve as an undergraduate researcher at the Natural Language Learning Lab under the supervision of Prof. Hyun-je Song, where I investigate knowledge graph‚Äìenhanced retrieval-augmented generation (RAG) architectures for improving factual accuracy and reasoning capabilities in LLMs.

My research interests center on developing graph-based retrieval pipelines that integrate structured knowledge with neural embedding spaces, implementing multi-hop reasoning mechanisms over knowledge graphs, and conducting ablation studies on embedding integration techniques. I am also exploring Embodied AI applications, particularly in IoT sensing systems and edge computing architectures.

I am actively preparing for graduate studies and am particularly interested in structuring research problems that bridge theoretical advances with practical, real-world applications. I welcome opportunities to discuss research collaborations, potential graduate programs, or technical discussions in AI and NLP.
